COZY BEAN CAFE

Nestled in the heart of bustling Kerico, just off Green gardens, "The Cozy bean" offers a tranquil escape from the town's vibrant chaos. With its warm, exposed brick walls, soft jazz melodies, and the rich aroma of freshly brewed Kenyan coffee, the caf√© exudes a cozy, inviting ambiance. Patrons sink into plush armchairs, their conversations a gentle hum against the backdrop of clinking ceramic cups. Once a modest bookshop, the space has been transformed into a haven for writers, artists, and those seeking a moment of peace, its shelves now lined with vintage coffee grinders and framed black and white photographs depicting Kericho's bygone era.
